# Global Economy System

The economy system in Vermeil is a global, multi-currency RPG experience. Earn coins, collect rare creatures, join clans, and climb the global leaderboards.

## Overview

Unlike most bots, Vermeil's economy is **global**. Your balance, items, and progress follow you across every server the bot is in. The system uses a dedicated high-performance database cluster to ensure fast and reliable transactions.

### Currencies
- **🪙 Coins**: The primary currency for everyday items and gambling.
- **💎 Gems**: A premium currency earned through rare activities or level-ups.
- **🔮 Tokens**: Special tokens used for high-tier upgrades and clan activities.

## Getting Started

1. Use `!start` to create your global profile and receive your starter pack (500,000 coins + 5,000 gems).
2. Use `!daily` and `!weekly` to claim your regular rewards.
3. Check your status anytime with `!balance` or `!profile`.

## Jobs & Career
You can climb the career ladder by applying for better jobs as you gain experience. Use `!jobs` to see requirements and `!work` every hour to earn your salary. High-tier jobs require specific items or a certain amount of total career earnings.

## RPG Battles
Capture creatures using `!hunt` and build a powerful team with `!team`. Once your team is ready, use `!battle` to challenge other players or face legendary bosses for massive rewards.
